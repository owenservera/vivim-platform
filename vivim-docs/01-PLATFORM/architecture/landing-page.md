# VIVIM: Your Personal AI Memory Platform

## Own, Share, Evolve Your AI

VIVIM is an open-source, decentralized AI memory platform that puts users in complete control of their AI systems. Built on blockchain technology with peer-to-peer networking, CRDT synchronization, and end-to-end encryption, VIVIM enables you to truly own, securely share, and continuously evolve your AI companions.

### 🔐 Own Your AI
Maintain full sovereignty over your AI systems and data. No central authority, no vendor lock-in – your AI memories are yours alone.

### 🔗 Share Your AI
Securely share AI configurations, knowledge bases, and experiences with trusted peers or communities while maintaining granular access controls and encryption.

### 📈 Evolve Your AI
Continuously improve and adapt your AI through decentralized collaboration, model fine-tuning, and community-driven enhancements.

### 🚀 Key Features
- **Decentralized Architecture**: Built on LibP2P with CRDT sync for resilient P2P networking
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Military-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit
- **Modular SDK**: TypeScript toolkit for building VIVIM-compatible applications
- **Progressive Web App**: Modern React interface with offline capabilities
- **Blockchain Integration**: On-chain verification for trust and provenance
- **AI Provider Agnostic**: Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, local models, and more

### 💡 Who Is It For?
- Developers building privacy-first AI applications
- Individuals seeking sovereign control over their AI companions
- Communities collaborating on shared AI knowledge bases
- Enterprises requiring secure, customizable AI infrastructure

### 🛠️ Technology Stack
**Frontend**: React 19, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Vite, Framer Motion, Zustand  
**Backend**: Bun Runtime, Express 5, Prisma ORM, PostgreSQL, Redis, Socket.IO  
**Decentralized Layer**: LibP2P, Yjs CRDT, WebRTC, WebSockets, @noble/crypto  
**AI Integration**: OpenAI SDK, Anthropic SDK, local LLM support

### 📦 Getting Started
```bash
# Clone and install
git clone https://github.com/owenservera/vivim-app.git
cd vivim-app
bun run setup:deps

# Configure environment
cp .env.example .env
# Edit .env with your settings

# Run development services
bun run dev
```
