## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

### What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes, 14 days free. No credit card required to start.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. Export everything anytime in JSON, Markdown, or PDF format.

### Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes. Early-stage startups (under $1M funding, under 2 years) qualify for 50% off Pro for the first year. [Apply here →](/startup-discount)

### Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Contact billing@vivim.app.

### What about student discounts?

Students and educators get Pro features free. [Verify your academic status →](/edu)
