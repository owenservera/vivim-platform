# Sharing Knowledge

VIVIM makes it easy to share your AI knowledge with friends, colleagues, or the community. When you share a conversation, recipients get access without needing your AI accounts.

---

## Sharing a Conversation

### Step-by-Step

1. Open any conversation
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Choose sharing method:
   - **Share Link** - Copy URL to share
   - **QR Code** - Show QR for others to scan
   - **Native Share** - Use device share (mobile)

---

## Share Options

### Expiration
Set when the link expires:
- **1 hour** - Quick access
- **24 hours** - Day access
- **7 days** - Week access
- **30 days** - Month access
- **Never** - Permanent access

### Reshare Permission
Choose if recipients can reshare:
- **Allow reshare** - Recipients can share with others
- **No reshare** - Single-use only

---

## Receiving Shared Content

When someone shares with you:

1. Open the share link
2. VIVIM shows what's being shared
3. Review the content
4. Tap **Save** to add to your library
5. Opens in your library

### Expired Links
If a link has expired:
- Sender must create a new link
- Ask sender to share again

---

## Share Formats

### Link
```
https://vivim.app/receive/[code]
```

Anyone with the link can view (unless expired).

### QR Code
Generate a QR code for easy mobile sharing. Recipients scan with their camera.

---

## Privacy

### What Recipients See
- Original
- Conversation messages timestamps
- Provider information

### What They Don't See
- Your AI account details
- Your other conversations
- Your library

---

## Use Cases

### Sharing with Team
- Project discussions
- Code reviews
- Design decisions

### Sharing with Friends
- Interesting AI conversations
- Learning resources
- Useful prompts

### Knowledge Transfer
- Onboarding new team members
- Documenting decisions
- Creating shared knowledge bases

---

## Managing Shared Content

### As Recipient
- Saved conversations appear in your library
- Marked as "Shared with you"
- Can be organized like any other conversation

### As Sender
- No central management currently
- Links can be revoked by expiration
- Consider setting expirations for sensitive content
