## INTRODUCTION

This package contains the complete founding documentation for VIVIM DAO - a fully decentralized, AI-assisted, user-sovereign knowledge management platform.

The founding documents establish:
- **Governance** - How decisions are made
- **Legal Framework** - Corporate structure and compliance
- **Economic Model** - Tokenomics and value distribution
- **Community Framework** - How members participate and earn

## DOCUMENT SUMMARIES

### 1. DAO Charter

**Purpose:** Supreme governance document establishing the DAO's constitutional framework.

**Key Contents:**
- Governance bodies and their powers
- Proposal types and voting thresholds
- Treasury management
- Member rights and duties
- Dispute resolution
- Amendment process

**Authority:** Highest - all other documents must conform

**Amendments:** 66% approval with 30% quorum

---

### 2. Articles of Digital Association

**Purpose:** Legal framework for the DAO as a corporate entity.

**Key Contents:**
- Corporate formation and registration
- Token structure (VGT, VWT)
- Membership and transfer rules
- Financial provisions
- Liability and indemnification
- Dissolution procedures

**Authority:** Legal compliance layer

**Jurisdiction:** Malta or Switzerland recommended

---

### 3. Tokenomics Founding Document

**Purpose:** Economic framework for token distribution and value capture.

**Key Contents:**
- Token specifications (VGT, VWT)
- Initial distribution (100M total)
- Vesting schedules
- Revenue allocation (60% to users)
- Dividend distribution model
- Governance rights

**Authority:** Economic layer

**Key Metrics:**
- Total Supply: 100,000,000 VGT
- Community Treasury: 40%
- Founding Team: 20%
- User Dividends: 15%
- Revenue Share: 60%

---

### 4. Community Operating Agreement

**Purpose:** Operational framework for community participation.

**Key Contents:**
- Membership tiers and benefits
- Contribution tracking system
- Bounty and grant programs
- Reputation system
- Dispute resolution
- Code of conduct

**Authority:** Community operations

**Key Programs:**
- Bounty Program
- Grants Program
- Milestone Contributions
- Reputation System

## KEY RELATIONSHIPS

### Governance ↔ Legal
- DAO Charter sets governance authority
- Articles provide legal implementation
- Conflicts resolved in favor of Charter

### Governance ↔ Economics
- Tokenomics defines token utility
- DAO Charter defines governance rights
- Treasury Committee oversees economic policy

### Governance ↔ Community
- Community Agreement implements governance programs
- Reputation system integrates with voting
- Moderation Council reports to governance

### Economics ↔ Community
- Tokenomics funds community programs
- Community contributions earn tokens
- Dividends reward community participation
