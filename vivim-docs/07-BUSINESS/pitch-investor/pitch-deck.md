# VIVIM Pitch Deck
## Decentralized AI Memory Platform

---

### Slide 1: Title & Vision
**VIVIM: Own, Share, Evolve Your AI**
- Open-source decentralized AI memory platform
- Blockchain-based with P2P networking and CRDT sync
- Mission: Put users in complete control of their AI systems
- Tagline: Your Personal AI Memory Platform

---

### Slide 2: The Problem
**Centralized AI Ownership Issues**
- Users don't own their AI models or data
- Vendor lock-in and lack of portability
- Privacy concerns with centralized AI providers
- No true collaboration or sharing capabilities
- Central points of failure and censorship risks

---

### Slide 3: Our Solution
**The VIVIM Architecture**
- Decentralized P2P network with LibP2P
- CRDT synchronization for real-time collaboration
- End-to-end encryption for all data
- Modular SDK for building AI applications
- Blockchain integration for trust and provenance
- AI provider agnostic architecture

---

### Slide 4: Key Features
**What Makes VIVIM Unique**
- 🔐 **True Ownership**: Users control AI and data
- 🔗 **Secure Sharing**: Encrypted collaboration with access controls
- 📈 **Continuous Evolution**: Community-driven AI improvement
- 🌐 **Resilient Network**: P2P architecture with no single point of failure
- 🔒 **Military-Grade Security**: E2E encryption at rest and in transit
- 🧩 **Modular & Extensible**: Plugin-based architecture for customization

---

### Slide 5: Technology Stack
**Built for Performance and Security**
- **Frontend**: React 19, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Vite
- **Backend**: Bun Runtime, Express 5, Prisma ORM, PostgreSQL
- **Decentralized**: LibP2P, Yjs CRDT, WebRTC, WebSockets
- **Security**: @noble/crypto, multiformats, uint8arrays
- **AI Integration**: OpenAI SDK, Anthropic SDK, local LLM support
- **Dev Tools**: Vitest, ESLint, Prettier, TypeScript
