## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

VIVIM's tokenomics is designed to create a sustainable, community-driven economy that:

- Rewards contributions and participation
- Aligns incentives between users, contributors, and the protocol
- Enables democratic governance
- Generates value for all stakeholders through revenue sharing

| Parameter | Value |

| Total Supply | 100,000,000 VGT |
| Initial Circulating | ~15% (at genesis) |
| Annual Inflation | 0% (fixed supply) |
| Revenue Share to Users | 50-70% |

### 7.1 Voting Power

| VGT Held | Voting Power Multiplier |
|----------|----------------------|
| 1-100 | 1.0x |
| 101-1,000 | 1.1x |
| 1,001-10,000 | 1.25x |
| 10,001+ | 1.5x |

*Multipliers apply to reputation-boosted voting only*

### 7.2 Quadratic Voting

For constitutional proposals, quadratic voting may be implemented:
```
Vote Weight = sqrt(VGT Held)
```

This prevents concentration of voting power.

## 8. DIVIDEND DISTRIBUTION

### 8.1 Dividend Eligibility

To receive dividends, a wallet must:
1. Hold at least 1 VGT at snapshot
2. Have completed identity verification (KYC-light) if required
3. Not be from a restricted jurisdiction

### 8.2 Dividend Calculation

Dividends are distributed based on:
- **Base Allocation**: Proportional to VGT held
- **Activity Bonus**: Additional for active protocol users
- **Tenure Bonus**: Additional for long-term holders

```
Dividend = (UserVGT / TotalEligibleVGT) × DividendPool × (1 + ActivityBonus + TenureBonus)
```

## 10. RISK FACTORS

### 10.1 Regulatory Risk
- Token may be classified as security in some jurisdictions
- Mitigation: Geographic restrictions, legal opinions

### 10.2 Market Risk
- Token value may be volatile
- Mitigation: Revenue-backed value, utility focus

### 10.3 Governance Risk
- Voting concentration in few hands
- Mitigation: Quadratic voting, reputation system

### 10.4 Economic Risk
- Insufficient revenue to fund operations
- Mitigation: Diversified revenue streams, reserves
