# VIVIM 90-Second Investor Pitch — Video Script

**Duration:** 90 seconds  
**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover  
**Target:** VC / Angel Investor Meeting

---

- [ ] Server running with full seed (320 conversations)
- [ ] PWA loaded and authenticated
- [ ] Graph pre-computed and rendering quickly

## The Script

### Scene 1: The Hook (0-15s)

**Visual:** Black screen. White text fades in slowly.

**On-screen text:**
```
Every AI power user lives here...
```

**Audio:** (silence — build anticipation for 3 seconds)

**Voiceover:**
> "Every AI power user lives here..."

**Action:** Show three browser tabs: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini. Each has multiple conversations open.

**Visual transition:** All three tabs close simultaneously with a satisfying "whoosh" sound effect.

**On-screen text:**
```
This is where their most valuable thinking goes to die.
```

**Voiceover:**
> "This is where their most valuable thinking goes to die."

---

### Scene 2: The Reveal (15-20s)

**Visual:** VIVIM logo animates in from center. Orange glow effect.

**On-screen text:**
```
VIVIM
Own Your AI Brain
```

**Voiceover:**
> "Introducing VIVIM."

---

### Scene 3: Magic #1 — Capture (20-35s)

**Visual:** Archive timeline view. Scroll from top to bottom showing 320 conversations.

**On-screen text (animated counter):**
```
320 conversations
9 AI providers
Fully organized
```

**Voiceover:**
> "One tap captures from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and six more providers. Your entire AI history — archived."

**Action:** Hover over a few conversations to show provider badges (ChatGPT green, Claude orange, Gemini blue).

---

### Scene 4: Magic #2 — Knowledge Graph (35-60s) ⭐

**Visual:** Click "Canvas" button. Graph animates into place with force-directed layout.

**On-screen text:**
```
2,161 insights
734 connections
Your AI brain, visible
```

**Voiceover:**
> "This is the money shot. Every conversation decomposed into atomic insights. Mapped. Connected."

**Action:** 
1. Zoom into largest cluster (database optimization)
2. Click a node to show ACU detail popup
3. Show relationship edges lighting up

**Voiceover (continued):**
> "This isn't a search tool. It's a second brain."

**Pause:** Let this sink in for 2 seconds.

---

### Scene 5: Magic #3 — Context Engine (55-70s)

**Visual:** Navigate to Context Cockpit. Show 8 layers loading one by one.

**On-screen text (layer counter):**
```
8 memories loaded
5 topic profiles active
5 entities referenced
```

**Voiceover:**
> "New chat. Eight layers of context assemble automatically. Memories. Topics. Entities."

**Action:** Type "Should I use microservices?" into chat input.

**Voiceover (continued):**
> "It knows you. Not because you told it..."

---

### Scene 6: The Payoff (70-80s)

**Visual:** AI response appears with citations highlighted.

**On-screen text:**
```
Grounded in your history
3 conversations cited
```

**Voiceover:**
> "...because it's been watching you think."

**Action:** Hover over citations to show source conversations.

---

### Scene 7: The Close (80-90s)

**Visual:** Fade to black. VIVIM logo appears in center with tagline.

**On-screen text:**
```
VIVIM
Own Your AI Brain
vivim.app
```

**Voiceover:**
> "That's VIVIM. You own your AI brain. Fully."

**Action:** Hold on logo for 3 seconds.

**Audio:** (silence)

**On-screen text (small, bottom):**
```
[Stop. Let them ask questions.]
```

## Post-Production Notes

### Music & Sound

- **Background:** Minimal ambient pad (no lyrics)
- **Sound effects:**
  - Tab close: "whoosh" (0:12)
  - Graph animate: subtle "power up" (0:38)
  - Citations appear: soft "ping" (1:12)

### Pacing

- **0-15s:** Slow, deliberate
- **15-35s:** Moderate pace
- **35-60s:** Slow down for graph (let it breathe)
- **60-90s:** Build to close, then pause

### Color Grading

- Maintain dark theme (#0a0a0f background)
- Boost orange (#EF9F27) for emphasis
- Keep contrast high for readability

## Backup Plan

If live demo fails:

1. **Graph doesn't render:** Cut to pre-recorded graph clip (1080p)
2. **Search fails:** Use pre-verified queries only
3. **Server lags:** Blame "demo gods", laugh, refresh
4. **Auth fails:** Have incognito backup ready
