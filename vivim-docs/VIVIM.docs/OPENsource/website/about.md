# About VIVIM

## Own Your AI Story

Every day, you have conversations with AI that change how you work, think, and create. Yet 73% of ChatGPT users have lost important conversations forever. Your ideas, code, research, and insights vanish when browser tabs close or accounts get suspended.

**VIVIM exists to change that.**

---

## Our Story

### The Problem We Saw

In early 2023, our founder was building AI-powered products and kept hitting the same wall: every conversation with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini disappeared after the session ended. Months of valuable work — code snippets, architectural decisions, research insights — gone in an instant.

The big AI companies had no incentive to fix this. Their business models depend on owning your conversations, not letting you own them yourself.

So we built VIVIM.

### What We Believe

**You should own your AI story.**

That means:

- Your conversations are yours, not the AI company's
- Your memories should persist beyond any single platform
- Your data should work for you, not against you
- Privacy isn't a feature — it's a human right

### The Tipping Point

The memory problem is getting worse:

- **500 million** people use AI assistants regularly
- Average knowledge worker uses **3+ AI tools** daily
- **Zero solutions** exist for cross-platform AI memory
- Every day, valuable insights disappear into the void

---

## What We Build

VIVIM is your personal AI memory platform — the system that remembers what AI forgets.

### Five Core Capabilities

1. **Sovereign History** — Capture conversations from every AI assistant. One unified archive you own completely.

2. **Dynamic Intelligence** — Your AI doesn't just remember. It understands. Automatic memory extraction turns conversations into actionable knowledge.

3. **Hyper-Secure Collaboration** — Share insights without sharing access. End-to-end encryption means you control what leaves your devices.

4. **Zero-Knowledge Privacy** — Even we can't see your data. Military-grade encryption with keys that never leave your device.

5. **Decentralized Network** — Your memories, your node. Run your own infrastructure or use ours. Either way, you're in control.
