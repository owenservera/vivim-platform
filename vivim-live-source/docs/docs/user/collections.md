# Organizing with Collections

Collections help you group related conversations together. Instead of scrolling through your entire library, you can organize conversations by project, topic, or any category that makes sense to you.

---

## Creating a Collection

1. Go to **Collections**
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Project Alpha", "React Tips")
4. Choose a color for the collection
5. Tap **Create**

---

## Adding Conversations to Collections

### Method 1: From Conversation View
1. Open a conversation
2. Tap the **Share/More** menu
3. Select **Add to Collection**
4. Choose the collection

### Method 2: From Collections Page
1. Go to **Collections**
2. Select a collection
3. Tap **Add Conversations**
4. Select conversations to add

---

## Collection Features

### Custom Colors
Choose from 9 preset colors:
- Indigo, Purple, Blue, Cyan, Green
- Amber, Red, Pink, Slate

### Rename & Delete
- Tap the **...** menu on any collection
- **Edit** to rename
- **Delete** to remove (conversations stay in library)

---

## Collection Views

When you open a collection, you see:
- Collection name and color
- Conversation count
- List of all conversations in the collection
- Ability to remove conversations

## Tips

1. **Create collections early** - Makes organization easier as your library grows
2. **Use consistent naming** - "Project X" or "Topic: X" 
3. **Color code** - Use colors to categorize (e.g., all projects = blue)
4. **Regular review** - Clean up collections quarterly
